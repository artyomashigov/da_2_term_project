# Literacy Rate Regression Analysis

Data Analysis 2 term project studying the relationship between student-teacher ratios and adult literacy rates.

## Project Goal

The project asks whether student-teacher ratios are associated with adult literacy rates after accounting for broader education and economic factors. It treats literacy as an educational outcome and compares it with school-resource and country-level indicators.

## Files

- `Literacy_rate_term_project_artyom_ashigov.ipynb` - full notebook with data preparation, regression analysis, and interpretation.
- `Literacy_rate_term_project_artyom_ashigov.html` - rendered notebook output.
- `Literacy_rate_term_project_artyom_ashigov.pdf` - final written report.
- `literacy_rate_raw_data.csv` - raw dataset.
- `literacy_rate_cleaned_data.csv` - cleaned dataset used for modeling.

## Variables

The analysis focuses on adult literacy rates and student-teacher ratios, with controls for government education expenditure, GDP per capita, primary education completion, and average years of schooling.

## Workflow

1. Load and clean the raw literacy and education data.
2. Prepare a modeling dataset.
3. Estimate regression models with education and economic controls.
4. Interpret the role of student-teacher ratio in the broader context.
5. Summarize results in a final report.

## Tools

Python, pandas, statsmodels, Jupyter Notebook, and regression diagnostics.
